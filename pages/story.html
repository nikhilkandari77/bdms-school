If there was only a mother living alone in the house, there were two verandahs and two kitchens and there was an extra room store room next to it, where we made our living, in the other two rooms, I started school when school started. It was then that I got a lot of support from the villagers. The dreams of the villagers put almost all their children to school. School started on 1 April 2017 and admission in the first year school was 30 children.

    After that because I used to teach tuition in Shyampur Rishikesh, whatever I earned from tuition I used to spend tuition on school.

    After the construction of the school gradually, because when the school was reopened again in 2018, then there were more children in it, then I thought you would not do it in two rooms. 50 to 60 children could not come in 2 rooms, so started the work of building a new building. Slowly moved forward today the school has 6 rooms and a van which brings children from the village.
