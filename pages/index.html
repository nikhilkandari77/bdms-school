    Sponsor-a-child


    Bishamber dutt memorial We empower children from the slums of India to reach their highest potential by providing free K-12 education in English, as well as providing the nutrition and healthcare, and extra-curricular activities needed to succeed. In addition to free education, we provide free computer technology training that sets them apart from others. This combination enables them to find jobs, work in our Tech Business Center, support their families, give back to the school and community, and break the cycle of poverty. We accept volunteers year-round to support our work.

    Donate


    We are currently fundraising to buy land and start construction for our Tech Business Center, set to open in 2022 in Rishikesh, India. You can contribute by buying a Brick and your name will be featured on a beautiful donation wall at the entrance of the building! The Tech Business Center will offer a wide-range of technology consulting and development services and will provide our graduates a clear pathway to successful internship, job placement, and experience. is a registered non-profit organization in the United States and India.



    " Bishamber Dutt Memorial School
    is a non-profitable school located in Tehri Garhwal village. Vikas Chandra Rayal is the founder of this school. The main objective of this school is to provide quality education to the children of Gular Dogi village as well as others who are not financially sound. We also provide education to those students who are very poor and we provide them education without any value. We are committed to contribute to the development of Gular Dogi village.
